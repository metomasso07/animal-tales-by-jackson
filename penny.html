Meet Penny, a gentle panda who lived in the misty bamboo forest with her two cubs.

One sunny morning, Penny and her cubs wandered deep into the forest, looking for fresh bamboo to eat. Crunch, crunch, crunch! They loved munching on the tender green shoots.

Suddenly, they heard a rustling sound. A big tiger stepped out from behind the trees! Penny's heart raced.

"Hello," said the tiger in a surprisingly gentle voice. "I'm Terry. I don't mean any harm."

Penny was confused. Everyone said tigers were dangerous! But Terry seemed friendly.

"I'm actually a vegetarian tiger," Terry explained. "I only eat plants and bamboo, just like you! Other animals are usually too scared to talk to me because of how I look."

Penny felt bad for judging Terry by his appearance. "I'm sorry I was scared," she said. "Would you like to share some bamboo with us?"

Terry's eyes lit up. "Really? That would be wonderful!"

From that day on, Penny and Terry became good friends. Penny learned that you shouldn't judge someone by how they look‚Äîwhat matters is what's in their heart.

Fun Fact: Pandas eat bamboo for up to 14 hours a day! They need to eat a lot because bamboo doesn't have many nutrients.
