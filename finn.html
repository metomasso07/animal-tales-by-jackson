Meet Finn, a bright orange fish who lived in a beautiful coral reef with his wife and eight baby fish. Finn loved his big family!

One morning, Finn woke up extra hungry. His tummy rumbled, and so did his wife's and all eight babies' tummies too! "We need food!" chirped the little fish.

"I'll find us breakfast," said Finn bravely. He swam out of their cozy coral home to search for tasty algae and tiny shrimp. But as he rounded a big rock, Finn gasped‚Äîa huge shark was swimming nearby!

Finn's heart raced. He darted back home as fast as his fins could carry him. "There's a shark out there!" he told his family.

But then something surprising happened. The shark poked his head around their coral. "Hi, neighbors!" he said with a big, toothy smile. "I just moved here. Want to play?"

The shark wasn't dangerous at all‚Äîhe only ate plankton! Finn and his family spent the whole day playing hide-and-seek in the reef with their new friend. After a fun day of games, they all went home for a delicious dinner together.

Fun Fact: Fish are covered in shiny scales that protect their bodies, and there are over 34,000 different types of fish in the world!
