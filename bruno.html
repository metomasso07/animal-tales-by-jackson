Bruno the Baby Boar

Story and art by Jackson, age 8

Meet Bruno, a curious baby boar with fuzzy brown fur and a little pink nose.

One sunny morning, Bruno asked his mama, "Can I go explore the forest? I want to see what's out there!"

His mama smiled. "Of course, but stay close to me."

Bruno and his mama trotted through the trees, sniffing the air and looking for acorns. Suddenly, Bruno stopped. "Mama, look!"

Right ahead of them stood a pack of wolves! Their golden eyes gleamed in the shadows. Bruno's heart pounded. Wolves were big and had sharp teeth!

The biggest wolf stepped forward. Bruno hid behind his mama, trembling.

But then something amazing happened. The wolf leader wagged his tail! "Hello, friends," he said gently. "We're just looking for berries. Would you like to help us?"

Bruno peeked out. The wolves weren't scary at all‚Äîthey were friendly! Bruno and his mama spent the afternoon picking berries and playing with their new wolf friends.

Sometimes the scariest-looking creatures turn out to be the kindest!

Fun Fact: Baby boars are called piglets! They're born with fuzzy hair and cute stripes that help them blend into the forest. As they grow up, their stripes fade away.
